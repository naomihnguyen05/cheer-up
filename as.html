Artist Statement

There are many days in the year when we often feel down and under the weather. With this project, I wanted to create a site with a few positive affirmations for those individuals. I also wanted to highlight my illustrations and create an interactive site. I was very interested in the idea of creating a parallax. Instead of creating a scrolling one, I decided on studying the idea of a 3D hover parallax. You can experience the hover effect by hovering your cursor over the cards and quotes. I hope that those that visit this site, enjoys their experience and feel better about their day!

- Naomi H. Nguyen
